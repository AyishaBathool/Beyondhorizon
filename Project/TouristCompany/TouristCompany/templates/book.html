    • Explore Geneva, a cosmopolitan city by the shores of Lake Geneva. Visit international organizations, museums, and the famous Jet d'Eau fountain.
    • Wander through Bern, the capital city with a UNESCO World Heritage old town. Explore the Zytglogge clock tower and the Bear Park.
    • Experience the charm of Lausanne, located on the shores of Lake Geneva. Visit the Olympic Museum and explore beautiful vineyards.
    • Immerse yourself in the grandeur of the Swiss Alps. Hike through lush meadows, gaze at the Matterhorn, and cruise on Lake Geneva.

Tourism Package: Explore the Beauty of the Maldives

Escape to the tropical paradise of the Maldives with our "Explore the Beauty of the Maldives" package. Experience the azure waters, pristine beaches, and vibrant marine life of this stunning island nation.

  • Duration: 10 days and 9 nights
  • Accommodation: Stay in luxurious overwater bungalows and beachfront villas that offer breathtaking ocean views.
  • Package Highlights:
    • Begin your journey in Malé, the capital city. Explore its bustling markets, historic mosques, and vibrant culture.
    • Visit Ari Atoll, a diver's paradise known for its diverse marine life. Dive or snorkel amidst colorful coral reefs and encounter manta rays and sea turtles.
    • Explore Baa Atoll, a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve. Witness vibrant coral reefs, go dolphin watching, and enjoy sustainable tourism practices.
    • Discover North Male Atoll, famous for water sports and luxury resorts. Experience underwater adventures and relax on white-sand beaches.
    • Indulge in the romantic ambiance of South Male Atoll. Enjoy scenic sunsets, overwater bungalows, and snorkeling in crystal-clear waters.
    • Experience the tranquility of Laamu Atoll. Engage in eco-friendly activities, explore local culture, and enjoy water sports.
    • Immerse yourself in the history of Addu Atoll. Visit the British Loyalty Island and explore the unique British influence.
    • Relax in Vaavu Atoll, a less crowded paradise. Enjoy pristine beaches, vibrant marine life, and tranquility.
    • Embark on an adventure to Alimatha Island for thrilling night snorkeling with manta rays and bird watching.
    • Unwind on Fihalhohi Island, perfect for budget travelers seeking relaxation and water sports.
  • Price: Starting from $1,750 per person (including airfare)
